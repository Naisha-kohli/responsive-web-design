Make Your Plants Thrive, Automatically.

LeafSense is an AI-assisted smart pot with moisture, light, and temperature sensors. It waters when needed and guides you with simple tips.

Why LeafSense?

Auto-Watering

Built-in reservoir and pump water your plant only when soil moisture drops below ideal levels.

Smart Sensing

Moisture, light, and temperature sensors continuously learn your plant’s needs.

Guided Care

Get simple, timely nudges: rotate for light, refill water, or adjust placement.

How It Works

Sensors → AI model → action. LeafSense reads moisture and light, predicts needs, and waters or notifies you.
